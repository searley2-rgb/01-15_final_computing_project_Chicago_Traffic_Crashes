    StreetStats Chicago

    Chicago drivers - Do you know how safe your streets really are?

    StreetStats delivers timley, accurate and well-informed traffic data that empowers drivers to make safer decisions on Chicago's roadways Using real crash data, the platform transforms complex information into interactive maps and visualizations that reveal where, when and why traffic risks occur.

    This visualization shows monthly traffic crashes across Chicago from 2018 to November 2025, highlighting long-term trends and seasonal patterns. Key Takeaways: Crash occurrences in Chicago exhibit a clear seasonal pattern, with peaks in the summer months when most drivers are on the road. There also appears to be a significant drop in crashes during March 2020, likely due to COVID-19 lockdowns reducing traffic volume. Several outliers in the data do exist, specifically a crash volume of 757 in February 2025. This could be attributed to unusally high temperatures that were conducive of better driving conditions. Overall, the data indicates that traffic safety initiatives should consider seasonal variations and external factors planning interventions.
